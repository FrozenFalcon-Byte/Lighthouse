About Lighthouse

What comes to your mind when you hear Lighthouse?
A lost ship, battling the furious waves of the sea, with people running all over the boat frantically, in a dark, dark night?
Or a concrete, red and white striped old tower, with a bright yellow light shining at the top, trying to guide lost ships to shore?
We see a team of incredibly creative people with a zeal to publish a wholesome college magazine! That's who we are!
The name Lighthouse is a testament to our explorative journey to find the best and most ingenious artforms, because we believe that it not only guides one from darkness to the transcendental enlightenment, but also helps to re-discover our truest passions in the process.
We at Lighthouse strive to make a small attempt from our side to collect these torches to create enough light to view the ornateness of life, which can be tough sometimes and one may feel lost in the endless pursuits of it.

Our Events in a Timeline

Jashn-e- Sadaa 2.0

22 April 2024 (12:00 PM to 6:00 PM)

Seminar Hall, E Building, VIIT Campus

After the grand success of Jashn-e-Sadaa last year, Lighthouse VIIT in collaboration with the Rotaract Club of VIIT, organized Jashn-e-Sadaa 2.0. It had two major goals this year. One, just as a Lighthouse guides ships in the stormy oceans and seas, we wanted to provide the students a platform, an opportunity to express the storms brewing in them, in the form of various artforms and represent themselves in the most beautiful and raw manner possible. The second being, Jashn-e-Sadaa, literally meaning the Celebration of Voice, celebrated and rejoiced the voice of the students through their poetry, the amazing performances depicted on the stage and more. The theme of this year for the event was, ‘#firse_kalam_se_zubaan_tak’. The special factor was VIIT’s own music band, one of its kind, the D-Chord.

Kalaasagar

3rd November 2023 (3:30 PM to 5:45 PM)

101, D Building, VIIT Campus

Kalaasagar was an Open-Mic event held by Lighthouse VIIT, a joint orientation/interactive event where participants and attendees were not only given information and an introduction about the club, but also a platform to express their creative side and their arts. The event ranged from many categories like poetry to stand-ups to live music and the response for the entire event was greatly positive and entertaining. The event was graced by the presence of Lighthouse’s faculty coordinator, Prof. Vivek Nagnath sir, and the Dean of Student Affairs, Dr. Radhika Purandare ma’am. They ignited the lamp and offered their reverence to the idol of Maa Saraswati, the Goddess of Knowledge, marking the inauguration of the event. They also addressed the crowd with a few words of encouragement, after which the performances began.

Jashn-e-Sadaa

2nd December 2022 (3:15 PM to 6:45 PM)

Seminar Hall, E Building, VIIT Campus

Jashn-e-Sadaa was an inquisitive open-mic collaborative event conducted by VIIT’s Poetry Club and Lighthouse, held on the backgrounds of hearing everybody's voice. Jashn means celebration and Sadaa meaning voice, blends up to mean a celebration of voices. It was a power packed event which was home to poetry, singing, rapping, instrumentals, stand-up comedy and much more to count for. The tagline of this event was ‘#Kalam_Se_Zubaan_Tak’, a testimony to portray a podium where every creation of would be appreciated and listened with no judgements.
